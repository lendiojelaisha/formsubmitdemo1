Our Services

Here are some of the services we provide, we look forward to taking care of you!

Dental Filling or Pasta

Is a way to restore a damaged tooth by decaying back to its normal function and shape. Fillings are used to repair cracked or broken teeth that have been word down from mis-use like nail-biting.

Oral Prophylaxis

Performed to help reduce the risk of gum and tooth disease. This procedure is recommended to be taken every 6 months or yearly, depending on a patient's history.

Flouride Theraphy

Flouride's primary benefit is that it helps in preventing tooth decay. This theraphy makes teeth remain healthy. This kind of treatment is important especially for children under the age of six.

Tooth Extraction

Procedure which your tooth will be completely removed from its socket. Tooth extraction will either be simple or surgical, depends whether your tooth visible or impacted.

Clear Aligners

A removable trays worn by the patient that will gradually move the teeth towards a more desirable position. It is free from wires & brackets that could bring discomfort to the patient.

Metal Braces

These are what most people picture when ther hear the word "braces". However, modern brackets are smaller and less noticeable. Metal braces is the most affordable among all the options.

Ceramic Braces

Are the same size as metal braces, except that they have tooth-colored brackets that blend in to teeth. Some use tooth-colored wires to be less noticeable. This is more expensive compared to metallic braces.

Sapphire Braces

Are crystal clear braces made from monocrystalline sapphire, these are highly esthetic because the tooth itself is more visible than the metal and ceramic brace.
